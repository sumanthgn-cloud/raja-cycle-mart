Since 1998

Raja Cycle Mart is a family-run workshop for everyday riders.

We started with a puncture stand and a single rack of tyres. Today, we serve commuters, students, delivery teams, and parents from across Tumkur.

Our promise

We keep things simple: clear prices, honest advice, and fast service. Riders can see what’s being repaired, ask questions, and take home maintenance tips.

  • Quick fixes for punctures and brake issues
  • Detailed tune-ups with before/after explanation
  • Stock tyres, tubes, and spares so riders don’t wait
  • Helpful blog posts for self-care between visits

Meet the team

Mr. Shaber leads the workshop with two senior technicians and an apprentice. Each technician is trained to explain work in plain words.

Need help in Kannada, Hindi, or English? We’re happy to switch languages so every rider feels heard.
