OUR STORY

A legacy of trust.

Raja Cycle Mart was founded in 1987 with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable cycle repairs to the people of Tumkur. Over the last 38 years, we've seen generations of cyclists come through our doors.

We believe that a well-maintained cycle is the key to a healthy, happy life. Whether you ride for commute, fitness, or fun, we are here to ensure your wheels keep turning smoothly.
