Top 5 Cycling Places in Tumkur

Explore the best scenic routes, hill climbs, and lake-side trails around Tumkur.

Tumkur is a paradise for cyclists, offering a perfect mix of challenging hill climbs, serene lake-side roads, and lush green forests. Whether you're a beginner looking for a peaceful ride or a pro seeking an intense workout, here are the top 5 places to pedal in and around our city.

1. Devarayanadurga (DD Hills)

Distance: ~15km from City Center | Difficulty: Hard

The ultimate challenge for every Tumkur cyclist. The climb to DD Hills offers steep gradients and hair-pin bends that will test your endurance. The reward? Breathtaking views from the top and a thrilling descent. Best visited early morning to enjoy the fog and cool breeze.

2. Namada Chilume & Bamboo Forest

Distance: ~12km from City Center | Difficulty: Moderate

If you love riding through nature, the road to Namada Chilume is a must. The trail passes through a beautiful bamboo forest and deer park. The roads are well-paved with gentle rolling hills, making it perfect for an intermediate Sunday morning ride.

3. Mydala Lake Road

Distance: ~18km from City Center | Difficulty: Moderate

Mydala Lake offers one of the most scenic backdrops in the district. The approach road is relatively flat but very peaceful, with farmlands on both sides. It's a great spot for a weekend breakfast ride with your cycling group.

4. Amanikere Lake Promenade

Distance: Within City | Difficulty: Easy

Perfect for beginners and families. The promenade around Amanikere Lake is flat, safe, and away from heavy traffic. It's an excellent place to start your cycling journey or for a quick evening loop after work.

5. Siddaganga Mutt Route

Distance: ~8km from City Center | Difficulty: Easy

The road leading to the historic Siddaganga Mutt is wide and generally has good lighting. It's a favorite for many city riders for their daily 1-hour morning routine. The spiritual vibe and the lush greenery around the mutt make it very rejuvenating.
