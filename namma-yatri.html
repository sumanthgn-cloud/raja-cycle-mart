How It Works

Customers will be able to request a home visit by sending:

  • Location (within S.S. Puram area)
  • Type of Issue (puncture, brake issue, chain slip, wobbling, etc.)
  • Photo or Video of the issue

Our team will then evaluate and confirm based on availability.

Transparent Pricing

🛵 Travel Charge (Distance-Based)

Charges depend ONLY on distance from Raja Cycle Mart, S.S. Puram:

Distance Travel Charge
0–1 km ₹20–₹30
1–2 km ₹40–₹50
2–3 km ₹60–₹70
3–4 km ₹80–₹100

? Travel charge is separate from repair or parts cost.

🛠️ Repair & Parts Cost (Separate)

Minimum Repair Value: ₹300+

To avoid loss and unnecessary travel, the repair job must meet a minimum value (e.g., Tire, tube, bell, stand, brake cable, chain replacement).

If the repair is too small and under ₹300 (like tightening small screws), we won–t travel unless combined with other paid work.

🔧 Fitting Charges – Separate

Fitting/installation charges apply separately. Policy: For Namma Yatrigalu home service, all accessories/parts (especially those above ₹300) must be purchased from Raja Cycle Mart. Boxes or parts from other shops are not permitted.

  • New Bell
  • Side-stand
  • Brake Shoe
  • Carrier
  • Mudguards
  • Tube or Tire

This keeps everything transparent and protects shop time & effort.
